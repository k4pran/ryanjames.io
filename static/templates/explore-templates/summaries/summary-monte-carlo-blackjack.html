Monte carlo is a simple technique that our agent can use to learn episodic tasks from experience rather than needing a model of the environment beforehand. This tutorial will show an agent that can learn blackjack, and will focus on the intuition behind the learning technique rather than a technical or mathematical dive into the monte carlo method itself.
